Today's Exercises

tap to expand · rest arms every other day

arms — 5 lb dumbbells
Stand feet shoulder-width, dumbbells at sides, palms forward. Curl up toward shoulders keeping elbows pinned. Lower slowly — that's where the work happens. Don't swing your back!
Hinge forward 45°, one hand on thigh, dumbbell in the other, elbow bent 90°. Extend arm straight back, squeeze at top, lower slowly. Targets the back of your arms — the key to sleek arms!
Stand tall, dumbbells at sides, slight elbow bend. Raise arms out to shoulder height like a T. Lower slowly, no jerking. These are harder than they look and so worth it for shoulder definition.
Dumbbells at shoulder height, elbows 90°, palms forward. Press straight overhead until arms nearly straight. Core tight, don't arch lower back. Lower with control.
Hold dumbbells at thighs, palms down. Raise both arms forward to shoulder height. Lower slowly. Pair with lateral raises for full shoulder coverage — chef's kiss combo.
core — no equipment
Forearms and toes, body straight head to heels. Hips don't sag or pike. Breathe. Start at 20-30 sec if needed and build up. This is the one daily non-negotiable — do it every single day!
On back, knees bent, fingertips behind ears (not pulling neck). Curl shoulders up, exhale at top, lower slowly. Abs only — no momentum.
Flat on back, legs straight, hands under hips. Raise legs to 90°, lower slowly and stop just before touching the floor. That hovering tension is the whole point — great for lower abs!
